What is Evapotranspiration (ET)?

Evapotranspiration (ET) is a measure of the combined processes of evaporation and plant transpiration. It is essential for understanding the water needs of crops and managing irrigation efficiently. The ET value helps in determining how much water is being lost to the atmosphere and can guide irrigation practices to ensure optimal crop growth.

Importance of ET in Agriculture

Monitoring ET allows farmers to make informed decisions about irrigation schedules and amounts. By aligning irrigation with ET values, farmers can optimize water use, reduce waste, and improve crop yields. Accurate ET sensing helps in managing water resources sustainably and achieving better crop productivity.
